# Trial vs No‑Trial (Fitness)

Hypothesis: A short trial increases initial conversions but may reduce paid retention vs a discounted annual plan without trial.

## Plan
- Two Offerings: A (7‑day trial), B (no‑trial + annual discount)
- Random split 50/50 on new users
- Paywall shown after first workout completion

## Metrics
- Trial start rate
- Paid conversion rate
- D7 and D14 retention
- Revenue per new user
