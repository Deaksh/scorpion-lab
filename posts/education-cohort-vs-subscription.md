# Education: Cohorts vs Subscriptions

A decision framework for education apps choosing between one‑time cohort pricing and recurring subscriptions.

## Summary
Subscriptions win when learners get fresh value every 2–4 weeks and habit loops drive retention. If you have 2 of the 3: ongoing value, habit loop, catalog depth, subscriptions usually outperform cohort fees.

- Offering A: cohort one‑time
- Offering B: monthly subscription
- Split new users 50/50
- Measure D30 retention and revenue per learner
